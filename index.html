About Me

Background & approach

I'm a writer and researcher focused on AI governance, technology policy, and equity. My work explores how AI and other emerging technologies impact those on the periphery of power.

Along with becoming an independent writer and researcher, I work in across academia and civil society organizations, commenting on regulatory frameworks and contributing to public consultations at both national and international levels.

I write for general audiences and policymakers. I believe those without formal training on AI still deserve to learn how it will shape their lives. Whether drafting briefing papers for legislators or long-form essays for curious readers, rigorous ideas don't require inaccessible language.
